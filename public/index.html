Galapago.org

Previous Next

Built in less than 2-1/2 weeks, Galapago.org is an end-to-end, third party platform that allows patients and doctors to exchange medical records ahead of scheduled appointments. Galapago greatly decreases patient wait time and increases clinical efficiency. Among the apps unique features is the doctor's ability to create their own, personalized intake forms, a dashboard to view aggregated patient data for a physician's entire clinic, and a patient's ability to save their own medical records in PDF format.

Technologies Utilized: PHP (Laravel framework), MySQL, JavaScipt, jQuery, Ignite PDF API, HL7 API, HTML5, Bootstrap and CSS3
